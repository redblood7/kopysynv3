Digital Delivery Policy

Instant Access to Your Courses

At Kopysynk Consulting, all our courses are delivered digitally, providing you with immediate access to your learning materials upon successful payment. This policy outlines how you will receive and access your purchased courses.

Course Delivery Process

Once your payment is confirmed, you will receive an email confirmation with a link to access your course. You can also access your purchased courses directly from your user account on our website.

Follow these steps to access your course:

  1. Log in to your account on the Kopysynk Consulting website.
  2. Navigate to the "My Courses" section of your dashboard.
  3. You will find a list of all your purchased courses. Click on the course title to start learning.

What You Will Receive

Your course purchase includes:

  • Lifetime access to the course materials, including all future updates.
  • Access to video lectures, presentations, and any supplementary materials such as workbooks or code samples.
  • Access to a community forum or Q&A section where you can interact with instructors and other students.

System Requirements

To access the courses, you will need a stable internet connection and a modern web browser (such as Chrome, Firefox, or Safari). Courses are accessible on desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.

Support and Assistance

If you encounter any issues with accessing your course, please contact our support team immediately at support@kopysynk.com. We are available to assist you with any technical difficulties to ensure you have a seamless learning experience.

No Physical Delivery

Please note that all our products are digital and delivered online. No physical products will be shipped to your address.
