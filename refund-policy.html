Refund Policy

Our Commitment to Your Satisfaction

At Kopysynk Consulting, we are committed to providing high-quality, effective training to all our customers. We stand behind the value of our courses and are confident that you will be satisfied with your learning experience. However, we understand that sometimes a course may not be the right fit.

Eligibility for a Refund

To be eligible for a refund, you must meet the following criteria:

  • You must request a refund within **14 days** of your purchase date.
  • You must not have completed more than **25%** of the course content. Course completion is tracked through our learning platform.
  • You must have a valid reason for the refund request, such as the course not meeting the description or having technical issues that we are unable to resolve.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact our support team at support@kopysynk.com with the following information:

  • Your full name and the email address used for purchase.
  • The name of the course you wish to refund.
  • The reason for your refund request.

Our team will review your request and get back to you within 3-5 business days. If your refund is approved, it will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment within 10 business days.

Non-Refundable Circumstances

Please note that we cannot issue refunds under the following circumstances:

  • The refund request is made more than 14 days after the purchase date.
  • You have completed more than 25% of the course.
  • You have been found to have violated our terms of service or engaged in any form of academic dishonesty.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our refund policy, please do not hesitate to contact us.
